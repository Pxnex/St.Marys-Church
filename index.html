St. Mary's Church Cheruvally

St. Mary's Church Cheruvally is a prominent Catholic church located in Cheruvally, Kerala, India. It is a well-known pilgrimage center dedicated to the Virgin Mary, attracting numerous devotees and visitors throughout the year. The church holds a significant place in the religious and cultural history of the region.

St. Mary's Church Cheruvally is celebrated for its annual feast, which draws large crowds of devotees from various parts of Kerala and beyond. The feast is marked by religious ceremonies, processions, and community gatherings, reflecting the deep faith and cultural traditions of the local Christian community.
